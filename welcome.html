Audible Library Scanner

Track your audiobook series and discover new releases

Getting Started

Navigate to Your Audible Library

Go to audible.com and sign in to your account. Navigate to your library page.

Open the Side Panel

Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar, or press Alt+Shift+A to open the scanner panel.

Start Scanning

Click "Start Scan" to analyze your library and find new books in your series.

Pro Tip

Enable "Scan on open" in Settings to automatically scan whenever you open the extension!
